Manatee Mentee User Manual

Welcome! This guide helps you find a mentor, manage your mentorship requests, and collaborate with your mentor through the Manatee mentoring platform.

Getting Started

Joining Manatee

  • Click Sign Up and select Mentee or Both (Mentor and Mentee)
  • Enter your email, create a strong password, and confirm your account
  • You will be logged in immediately and redirected to your Mentee Dashboard
  • Returning users can log in at any time using the login page

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For enhanced security, you can enable 2FA on your account:

  • Go to Profile Settings and select Enable 2FA
  • Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Authy, Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, etc.)
  • Enter the 6-digit code from your app to verify setup
  • On future logins, you will be prompted to enter a code from your authenticator app

Setting Up Your Profile

Complete Your Information

Important: Before you can search for mentors, you must set up your profile. Go to the Mentee Dashboard and fill in:

  • Personal Details: Title (optional), First Name, Surname, Job Title
  • Location: Your geographic location (required—mentors are matched by location)
  • Phone Number (optional): Provide if you're comfortable with mentors reaching out by phone

Click Update Profile to save. Once your profile is complete, you can start searching for mentors.

Finding and Requesting a Mentor

Search for Available Mentors

Click Find Mentors to discover available mentors in your area. To refine your search:

  • Select a Function: Choose the professional specialty you need help with (e.g., Operations, Finance, HR, Technology)
  • Browse Results: The system displays all available mentors in your location who specialize in that function
  • Review Details: Each mentor card shows their name, job title, and expertise

Send a Mentorship Request

  • Click Request Mentorship on a mentor's profile
  • Your request is sent to the mentor immediately
  • The mentor receives an email notification and can accept or decline
  • Your request appears in your Your Mentorships list with a "pending" status

What Happens Next?

  • You will receive an email when the mentor responds
  • If accepted, the relationship status changes to "accepted" and you gain access to the shared Next Step Record (goals page)
  • If declined, the mentor may include a message explaining why or offering alternative help

Managing Your Mentorship Requests

Your Mentorships List

Visit Your Mentorships to see all your mentorship relationships. Each row shows:

  • Mentor name and their job title
  • Function you requested help with
  • Status: Pending (waiting for mentor), Accepted (active), or Ended (completed)
  • Start date and last update time

Pending Request Actions

While a request is pending, you can:

  • Resend Request: Send a reminder to the mentor if they haven't responded (useful after 3-5 days)
  • Cancel Request: Withdraw your request if you no longer need mentoring. The mentor receives a cancellation notification.

Tip: Avoid sending multiple requests to the same mentor for the same function. One polite reminder is usually sufficient.

Active Mentorships and Goal Records

Collaboration Space

Once your mentor accepts, click the Goals button to enter your shared workspace called the Next Step Record. This is where you and your mentor collaborate, track progress, and share resources.

How to Use the Next Step Record

  • View the Log: All entries from both you and your mentor appear here, with the newest at the top
  • Add Entries: Create entries to share goals, action items, updates, questions, or resources with your mentor. You can:
    • Select an entry type: Goal, Action Item, Resource, or Update
    • Type your message (you can include URLs which will become clickable links)
    • Optionally attach a file (PDF, JPG, DOC, DOCX, up to 10MB)
  • Edit Your Entries: Click the pencil icon (✏️) on any of your entries to:
    • Change the entry text
    • Add, replace, or remove an attached file
  • Delete Entries: Click the trash icon (🗑️) to remove an entry. A confirmation will be shown before deletion.
  • Download Attachments: Click the paperclip icon (📎) to download files your mentor or you have shared

Entry Types

  • Goal: A long-term objective or milestone
  • Action Item: A specific task or next step to complete
  • Resource: A link, document, course, or tool to learn from
  • Update: Progress notes, questions, or general information

Unread Indicators

In your Mentorships list, a small badge on the Goals button shows how many new entries your mentor has added. The badge clears once you view the goals page.

Ending a Mentorship

When you've achieved your goals or need to close the relationship, click End Mentorship and select a reason from the list (e.g., "Goals Achieved", "No Longer Available", "Other"). Your mentor will be notified.

Email Notifications

You will receive email notifications for:

  • Welcome/Confirmation: Confirmation that your account was created or profile updated
  • Mentor Response: When a mentor accepts or declines your request (may include a message)
  • Mentorship Ended: When your mentor ends the relationship
  • New Goal Entry: When your mentor adds a new entry to your shared Next Step Record
  • Resend Reminders: If requested by admin (a friendly reminder to check if the mentor has responded)

All notification emails contain direct links to your dashboard for quick access.

Account Settings and Security

Updating Your Profile

Visit Profile Settings to:

  • Update your personal information (name, title, location)
  • Change your email address
  • Update your password
  • Enable or disable 2FA

Password Best Practices

  • Use a password at least 8 characters long
  • Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
  • Never share your password with anyone
  • If you suspect your account is compromised, change your password immediately

Best Practices for Getting the Most from Mentoring

  • Keep Your Profile Current: Update your location and job title so mentors can find and understand you. Mentors are more likely to accept requests from complete profiles.
  • Be Specific with Requests: Request mentoring for specific functions where you genuinely need help. Avoid requesting multiple mentors for the same goal unless absolutely necessary.
  • Be Patient: Mentors are busy professionals. Allow 3-5 days for a response before sending a reminder. Don't spam with repeated requests.
  • Engage Actively: Use the Next Step Record regularly. Add updates, ask questions, and share progress. Engaged mentees get more value from their mentors.
  • Come Prepared: When adding entries, be specific. Instead of "I'm stuck," try "I need help with X because Y. Here's what I've tried..."
  • Use Resources: If your mentor shares links or documents, review them and provide feedback. This shows you value their effort.
  • Set Clear Goals: Use the "Goal" entry type to establish what you want to achieve. Clear goals help both of you stay focused.
  • Respect Their Time: Mentors volunteer their expertise. Be courteous, avoid last-minute asks, and show appreciation for their guidance.
  • End with Gratitude: When finishing the mentorship, use the Next Step Record to thank your mentor and summarize what you learned.

Troubleshooting

I can't find the "Find Mentors" button

  • First, complete your profile (name, location). This is required to search.
  • Click Update Profile to save
  • Then return to the dashboard—the "Find Mentors" button should appear

A mentor declined my request but I didn't receive an email

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Visit your Your Mentorships list—the request status will show as "ended" with the decline reason
  • If your email is wrong, update it in Profile Settings

I sent a request but it disappeared from my list

  • The request may have been declined. Check the status in Your Mentorships.
  • Ended mentorships remain visible in your list for your records

I can't add entries to the Next Step Record

  • Check that the mentorship status is "accepted" (not "pending" or "ended")
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Ensure you're using a supported file format if attaching a file (PDF, JPG, DOC, DOCX, max 10MB)
  • If the issue persists, contact your administrator

I forgot my password

  • Click Forgot password? on the login page
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your email for a password reset link
  • Follow the link to create a new password

My mentor hasn't responded to my request

  • Wait 3-5 business days before resending
  • Click Resend Request to send a reminder
  • If still no response after another week, try requesting a different mentor
  • Some mentors may be temporarily unavailable—try again later

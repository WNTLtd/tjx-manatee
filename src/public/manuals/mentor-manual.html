Manatee Mentor User Manual

Welcome! This guide helps you manage your mentor profile, respond to mentee requests, and collaborate with your mentees through the Manatee mentoring platform.

Getting Started

Joining Manatee

  • Click Sign Up and select Mentor or Both (Mentor and Mentee)
  • Enter your email, create a strong password, and confirm your account
  • You will be logged in immediately and redirected to your Mentor Dashboard
  • Returning users can log in at any time using the login page

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

For enhanced security, you can enable 2FA on your account:

  • Go to Profile Settings and select Enable 2FA
  • Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Authy, Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, etc.)
  • Enter the 6-digit code from your app to verify setup
  • On future logins, you will be prompted to enter a code from your authenticator app

Setting Up Your Profile

Complete Your Information

Your profile helps mentees find you and understand your expertise. Fill in the following:

  • Personal Details: Title (optional), First Name, Surname, Email
  • Professional Details: Job Title, Phone Number (optional)
  • Location: Your geographic location (used to match with nearby mentees)

Availability and Expertise

The Mentor Dashboard contains key settings:

  • "Available to Mentor" Toggle: Turn this ON to appear in mentee search results. Turn it OFF when you're unable to take on new mentees.
  • Functions/Specialties: Select all the professional functions you can mentor (multi-select). Examples: Operations, Finance, HR, Technology. Only mentees seeking these functions will be matched to you.

Click Update Profile to save all changes. Your profile is immediately visible to mentees.

Managing Mentorship Requests

Viewing Incoming Requests

When mentees submit requests, they appear in the Incoming Mentee Requests section of your dashboard. You'll see:

  • Mentee name and email
  • Their location and the function they're seeking help with
  • Request date and time

You will also receive an email notification when a new request arrives.

Accepting a Request

  • Review the mentee's information
  • Click Accept to begin the mentorship
  • (Optional) Add a personal message to welcome the mentee or set expectations
  • The mentee receives an acceptance email with your message
  • Your new mentee appears in the Mentorships section as "accepted"

Declining a Request

  • If you cannot mentor this person, click Decline
  • (Recommended) Add a message explaining why or offering to help another way
  • The mentee receives your decline with your message
  • The request is archived and no longer appears in your inbox

Active Mentorships and Goal Records

The Mentorships List

Your dashboard shows all mentorships in chronological order with their status:

  • Pending: Request sent, awaiting mentor action (your action to accept/decline)
  • Accepted: Active mentorship currently underway
  • Ended: Completed or closed mentorship (appears in history)

For each mentorship, you'll see at a glance:

  • Mentee name
  • Status and when it started
  • A Goals button to access the collaboration space
  • An action button to end the relationship (for accepted mentorships)

Working with Next Step Records

Click the Goals button to open the Next Step Record—your shared workspace with your mentee. Here you can:

  • View the Log: See all notes and updates from both you and your mentee (newest entries at the top)
  • Add Entries: Create mentor-side notes, goals, action items, resources, or updates. You can type text and attach files (PDF, JPG, DOC, DOCX, up to 10MB)
  • Edit Entries: Click the pencil icon (✏️) on any of your entries to modify or remove attached files
  • Delete Entries: Click the trash icon (🗑️) to remove an entry (a confirmation will be shown)
  • Download Attachments: Click the paperclip icon (📎) to download files you or your mentee have shared

Unread Indicators

In your Mentorships list, a small badge on the Goals button shows how many new entries your mentee has added. Once you view the goals page, the badge clears.

Ending a Mentorship

When the mentorship is complete or must close, click End Mentorship and select a reason from the list (e.g., "Goals Achieved", "No Longer Available", "Other"). The mentee will receive a notification that the relationship has ended.

Email Notifications

You will receive email notifications for:

  • New Mentorship Request: A mentee has requested to work with you
  • Request Resend/Reminder: The mentee or admin has resent a request reminder (the mentee is still waiting for your response)
  • Request Cancelled: A mentee has withdrawn their pending request
  • Mentorship Ended: Your mentee has ended the relationship
  • New Goal Entry: Your mentee has added a new entry to your shared Next Step Record

These emails contain direct links to your dashboard for quick action.

Account Settings and Security

Updating Your Profile

Visit Profile Settings to:

  • Change your email address
  • Update your password
  • Modify personal or professional information
  • Enable or disable 2FA

Password Best Practices

  • Use a password at least 8 characters long
  • Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
  • Don't share your password with anyone
  • If you suspect your account is compromised, change your password immediately

Best Practices for Effective Mentoring

  • Keep Your Availability Accurate: If you're too busy to take on new mentees, turn off "Available to Mentor" temporarily. This prevents mentees from requesting if you can't help them promptly.
  • Update Your Expertise: As your professional focus changes, update your Functions/Specialties to match what you can realistically help with.
  • Respond Promptly: Try to accept or decline requests within 48 hours. Mentees are more likely to engage with responsive mentors.
  • Be Specific with Messaging: When accepting, set expectations. When declining, explain why and offer an alternative if possible.
  • Use Goal Records Consistently: Add regular notes, milestones, and resources to your Next Step Record. This creates a valuable record of the mentorship and helps both of you stay aligned.
  • Provide Actionable Feedback: When adding entries, be specific and constructive. Include links, documents, or resources where helpful.
  • End Respectfully: When finishing a mentorship, use a clear reason and send a brief message thanking your mentee for the experience.

Troubleshooting

I haven't received a request notification email

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Log into Manatee and check the Incoming Mentee Requests section to see if a request is pending
  • If your email is incorrect, update it in Profile Settings

A mentee says they sent a request but I don't see it

  • Verify that your "Available to Mentor" toggle is ON
  • Verify that the function they requested matches one of your selected Functions
  • If both are correct, ask the mentee to try requesting again

I can't add entries to the Next Step Record

  • Ensure the mentorship status is "accepted" (not "pending" or "ended")
  • Try refreshing the page
  • If the issue persists, contact your administrator

I forgot my password

  • Click Forgot password? on the login page
  • Enter your email address
  • Check your email for a reset link
  • Follow the link to create a new password
